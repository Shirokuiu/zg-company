Who we are?

Due to extensive experience in the field of aviation and logistics, with overall more than 15 years of experience on aviation market, carrying out long-term and short-term charter contracts, and working on different aviation projects around the globe.

Our goal is simple; We are planning to help and support young and small aviation companies, or start-up aviation companies, in order to increase their efficiency and their recognizability in the world of aviation.
